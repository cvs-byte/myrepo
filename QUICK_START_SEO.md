## 🚨 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### ❌ Don't:
- ❌ Stuff keywords unnaturally
- ❌ Buy backlinks or use PBN
- ❌ Create duplicate content
- ❌ Cloak content or redirect deceptively
- ❌ Change robots.txt without testing
- ❌ Abandon old URLs without redirects
- ❌ Neglect mobile optimization
- ❌ Ignore Core Web Vitals

### ✅ Do:
- ✅ Write naturally for humans first
- ✅ Build genuine backlinks through outreach
- ✅ Create unique, valuable content
- ✅ Be transparent with Google
- ✅ Test all changes before live
- ✅ Implement 301 redirects properly
- ✅ Optimize for mobile first
- ✅ Monitor performance metrics

## 💬 QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### Q: When will I see results?
**A:** First signals in 3-5 days. Measurable improvement in 2-4 weeks. Significant growth in 2-3 months.

### Q: How much will traffic increase?
**A:** Expected 20-60% within 3 months. Depends on competition, budget, and consistency.

### Q: Should I hire an SEO agency?
**A:** With this plan, you can see good results in-house. Consider hiring after 3 months if growth plateaus.

### Q: Is schema markup really important?
**A:** Yes! Google can't understand content without it. Schema = 10-15% CTR boost.

### Q: How often should I update content?
**A:** For blog posts: quarterly refreshes. For pages: monthly when possible.

### Q: What if I don't see results?
**A:** Check GSC for errors, verify schema implementation, assess competition, review content quality.
